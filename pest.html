🐛 Pest Control

Pest control is the process of managing and reducing harmful insects, weeds, and diseases that affect crop growth. Proper pest control helps in protecting crops and improving agricultural productivity.

Types of Pest Control:

• Biological Control – Using natural predators like ladybugs
• Organic Control – Using neem oil, compost sprays
• Chemical Control – Using pesticides (carefully)
• Mechanical Control – Removing pests manually or using traps

Common Pests:

• Aphids
• Caterpillars
• Whiteflies
• Beetles

Benefits:

• Protects crops from damage
• Improves crop yield
• Maintains crop quality
• Reduces economic loss

Tips for Farmers:

• Regularly check crops for pests
• Use natural methods first (like neem)
• Avoid excessive chemical use
• Rotate crops to reduce pest attack
