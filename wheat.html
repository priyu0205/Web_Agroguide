🌾 Wheat Information

Season: Rabi

Soil: Loamy Soil

Temperature: 10°C to 25°C

Description:

Wheat is one of the most important cereal crops in India. It is used to make flour for chapati, bread, and other food items.

Crop Details:

💧 Water: Requires 3–4 irrigations (not too much water).

🌾 Seeds: Use good quality certified seeds for better yield.

🚜 Weed Control: Remove weeds on time for healthy crop.

🌾 Harvest: Crop is ready when plants turn golden yellow.

📈 Benefit: Gives good production and income if managed properly.

🌍 Climate: Needs cool weather for growing and warm weather at harvest time.
