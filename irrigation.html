💧 Proper Irrigation

Proper irrigation is essential for healthy crop growth. It ensures that crops receive the right amount of water at the right time, which helps improve yield and prevents water wastage.

Types of Irrigation:

• Drip Irrigation – Supplies water directly to plant roots
• Sprinkler Irrigation – Water is sprayed like rainfall
• Surface Irrigation – Traditional method using canals

Benefits:

• Saves water
• Improves crop production
• Reduces soil erosion
• Prevents overwatering

Tips for Farmers:

• Water crops early morning or evening
• Avoid excess watering
• Use modern irrigation systems like drip
• Check soil moisture regularly
