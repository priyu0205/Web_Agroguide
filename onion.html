🧅 Onion Information

Season: Kharif

Soil: Black Soil

Temperature: 13°C to 24°C

Description:

Onion is an important vegetable crop in India. It is widely used in cooking for taste and flavor.

Crop Details:

🌍 Climate: Needs mild climate, not too hot or too cold.

💧 Water: Requires regular watering but avoid excess water.

🌾 Seeds: Use good quality seeds or healthy seedlings.

🌱 Soil: Grows well in well-drained loamy soil.

🚜 Weed Control: Remove weeds regularly for better bulb growth.

🐛 Pest Care: Protect from pests like thrips and diseases.

🌾 Harvest: Crop is ready when leaves turn yellow and fall down.

📈 Benefit: Gives good profit due to high market demand.
