☁️ Cotton Information

Season: Kharif

Soil: Black Soil

Temperature: 21°C to 30°C

Description:

Cotton is an important cash crop in India. It is used to make cloth, thread, and other textile products.

Crop Details:

🌍 Climate: Needs warm climate with plenty of sunlight.

💧 Water: Requires moderate water, avoid too much water.

🌾 Seeds: Use good quality hybrid seeds for better yield.

🌱 Soil: Grows best in black soil with good drainage.

🚜 Weed Control: Remove weeds regularly for healthy growth.

🐛 Pest Care: Protect from pests like bollworm and aphids.

🌾 Harvest: Crop is ready when cotton bolls open and become fluffy.

📈 Benefit: Gives good income as it is a valuable cash crop.
