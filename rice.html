🍚 Rice Information

Season: Kharif

Soil: Clay Soil

Temperature: 20°C to 35°C

Description:

Rice is one of the most important food crops in India. It is a staple food for many people and used in daily meals.

Crop Details:

🌍 Climate: Needs hot and humid climate for good growth.

💧 Water: Requires plenty of water and standing water in field.

🌾 Seeds: Use good quality and high-yield variety seeds.

🚜 Weed Control: Remove weeds regularly for better growth.

🌱 Soil: Grows well in clay or loamy soil that holds water.

🌾 Harvest: Crop is ready when grains become hard and golden.

📈 Benefit: Gives high production and is main source of food and income.
