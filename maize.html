🌽 Maize Information

Season: Kharif

Soil: Black Soil

Temperature: 18°C to 27°C

Description:

Maize is an important cereal crop in India. It is used as food for humans and also as feed for animals.

Crop Details:

🌍 Climate: Needs warm climate with good sunlight.

💧 Water: Requires moderate watering, avoid waterlogging.

🌾 Seeds: Use high-quality hybrid seeds for better yield.

🌱 Soil: Grows well in well-drained loamy soil.

🚜 Weed Control: Remove weeds early for proper growth.

🐛 Pest Care: Protect from pests like stem borer and armyworm.

🌾 Harvest: Crop is ready when cobs are fully developed and dry.

📈 Benefit: Gives good yield and is useful for both food and fodder.
